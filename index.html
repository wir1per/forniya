Some people are remembered like places. Safe places. Warm places. The kind you carry with you even when you are far away.

A glow that remains long after the moment has passed. A feeling that life, somehow, became more beautiful because you are in it.

And maybe that is the simplest truth of all: before you, beauty was something I noticed. After you, beauty became something I felt. In the air. In music. In silence. In hope. In every small future my heart dares to imagine.
